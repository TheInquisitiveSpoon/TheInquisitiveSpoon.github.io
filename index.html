Economy

The core mechanics of this demonstration revolve around a harvesting, selling and trading economy, where a player can harvest resources such as wood, stone and iron and buy, sell or trade resources with non-playable character merchants.

There are 3 harvestable resources in the game wood, stone and iron ore, as well as gold that acts as a currency. In real life these resources have worth based upon supply and demand, I have replicated this by making iron nodes much rare, as well as making them give the player less total resources per node.
